About Atlas Health Systems

Building practical healthcare technology for Ethiopia

We are developing modern electronic health record solutions that help hospitals improve patient care, simplify daily workflows, and securely manage health information.

Who We Are

A healthcare technology company focused on real hospital needs

Atlas Health Systems is focused on transforming the way hospitals and healthcare providers in Ethiopia capture, access, and manage patient information.

We believe healthcare technology should be reliable, accessible, secure, and designed around the people who use it every day.

Our approach is shaped by field research, conversations with healthcare professionals, and an understanding of the infrastructure challenges many hospitals face.

Our Purpose

Supporting better care through better information

Atlas is being designed to help healthcare organizations move from fragmented paper records to dependable digital workflows.

01

Our Mission

To empower healthcare providers in Ethiopia with secure, reliable, and easy-to-use digital tools.

02

Our Vision

A more connected healthcare system where providers can access accurate patient information.

03

Our Commitment

To build technology around local workflows, infrastructure, language needs, and hospital goals.

Why We Started

Many hospitals still depend on paper or disconnected systems

Paper records can be difficult to organize, retrieve, share, and protect. Existing software can also depend heavily on reliable internet access.

Atlas is working to address these challenges with technology designed for reliability, usability, and continuity of care.

Challenges We Address

Paper documentation Digitized
Limited internet Offline-ready
Disconnected departments Coordinated
Missing information Centralized
Complex workflows Simplified
