Atlas EHR Solutions

Connected digital tools for modern hospital care

Our solutions help hospitals manage patient information, improve clinical workflows, reduce paperwork, and continue essential operations during connectivity challenges.

Core Platform

Essential workflows for everyday hospital care

Atlas is prioritizing the foundational hospital and record-keeping workflows that provide the greatest immediate value to healthcare teams, while actively developing specialty-specific documentation and workflow tools for physicians across different areas of medicine.

01

Patient Registration

Register new patients, capture demographic information, search existing records, and reduce duplicate patient files.

02

Outpatient Visits

Document clinic visits, symptoms, assessments, diagnoses, treatment plans, and follow-up instructions.

03

Inpatient Admissions

Manage patient admissions, assigned units, attending providers, hospital stays, and ongoing care.

04

History and Physical

Record medical history, current symptoms, allergies, medications, physical findings, and relevant clinical background.

05

Nursing Notes

Document observations, vital signs, interventions, changes in condition, and ongoing nursing care.

06

Medication Orders

Record medication orders, doses, routes, frequency, instructions, allergies, and treatment history.

07

Laboratory Orders and Results

Create laboratory requests, track order status, record results, and make findings available to authorized providers.

08

Imaging Orders and Results

Manage imaging requests, examination details, reports, findings, and related clinical follow-up.

09

Diagnoses

Document working and confirmed diagnoses and connect them to visits, admissions, treatment plans, and patient history.

10

Discharge Summaries

Summarize hospital stays, diagnoses, treatments, medications, outcomes, and follow-up recommendations.

11

Basic Surgery Documentation

Record the procedure, surgeon, key findings, outcome, and essential postoperative information.

12

Longitudinal Patient Record

Bring visits, admissions, notes, medications, results, diagnoses, procedures, and discharge information together in one patient history.

Offline-Capable Workflows

Designed to keep essential hospital tasks moving

Atlas is being designed with local deployment and offline access in mind, allowing healthcare teams to continue key workflows when internet access is unavailable.

  • Register patients locally
  • View locally stored patient history
  • Write and save clinical notes
  • Record medications and prescriptions
  • Continue essential care workflows
  • Synchronize data when connectivity returns
