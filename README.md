# atlas-ehr
Atlas Health Systems

## Overview

A professional marketing website for a company that develops Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems for hospitals and healthcare providers in Ethiopia.

## About the Company

We build secure, reliable, and easy-to-use EHR solutions that help healthcare facilities improve patient care, reduce paperwork, streamline hospital operations, and manage medical information efficiently.

## Website Headline

**Modern Electronic Health Records for Ethiopian Healthcare**

## Website Purpose

This website provides information about:

- Our company
- Our EHR solutions
- Our services
- Our mission and values
- A little bit about us and how we can be contacted
